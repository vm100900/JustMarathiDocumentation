Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Just Marathi handles your data, including the use of camera, handwriting recognition, and screen usage analytics.

1. Camera Usage

Just Marathi uses your device’s camera to capture handwriting. Camera access is only activated when you choose to use handwriting recognition features.

2. Handwriting Recognition

Captured handwriting images are securely transmitted to the Google Cloud Vision API to extract text. Images are not stored by the app after processing and are not collected or saved on our servers.

3. Analytics (Screen Usage Tracking)

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how users interact with the app. This helps us improve the app experience by analyzing which screens are most visited.

Analytics data is collected anonymously and does not include personal information.

  • Data collected may include screen visits, feature usage, device info, and general crash events.
  • No handwriting or camera images are tracked through analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

Just Marathi uses the following services:

  • Google Cloud Vision API – for handwriting recognition.
  • Google Firebase Analytics – for anonymous usage tracking.

Learn more here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Google Cloud Vision Data Usage

5. Data Sharing and Storage

We do not store any camera images or handwriting data. All recognition is processed in real time and discarded immediately. Analytics data is stored by Firebase according to their retention policies.

6. User Consent

Camera and analytics permissions are requested explicitly. You can revoke these anytime through system settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Just Marathi does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted information, please contact us to request deletion.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests, contact us at: monkeynevermind@gmail.com

9. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally. We encourage users to review it periodically for any changes.
